I’m a Richmond-based journalist who prioritizes clean copy and improving newsrooms. I believe in style guides as living documents and proactive solutions for editorial teams.

Right now, I’m a copy editor at CNET. I’ve previously worked for Washington City Paper, Guns & America, WAMU 88.5/DCist, Slate, Mic and The Free Lance–Star, my hometown newspaper.

Since 2019, I’ve been a card-carrying union member of the Writers Guild of America, East.

In my spare time, I play a lot of video games.
